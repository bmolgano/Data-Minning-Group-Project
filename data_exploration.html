Data Exploration

This page highlights our exploratory data analysis and data understanding process. We combined multiple airline datasets from 2019, 2022, and 2024 to examine flight performance trends across major U.S. airports, focusing on delay frequency, duration, and causes.

Key Insights

  • Combined Dataset: 1,428 records across 21 variables covering Denver (DEN), LaGuardia (LGA), and Kansas City (MCI).
  • Normality Diagnostics: QQ plots revealed strong right skew in delay-related variables, suggesting log transformation.
  • Correlation Analysis: Highly correlated variables (r > 0.9) were identified between flight counts and delay durations.
  • Normalization: Features were standardized to mean = 0, std = 1 to prepare for modeling and clustering.

These insights guided our data preprocessing, normalization, and variable selection steps before moving to the modeling phase.
